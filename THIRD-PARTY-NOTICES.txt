THIRD-PARTY SOFTWARE NOTICES AND INFORMATION

CodeBrix.Platform.Fonts.Roboto is a CodeBrix-family redistribution of the
Roboto font family, packaged as a .NET 10 NuGet library. The package
incorporates one piece of third-party material:

  1. The Roboto font family (SIL OFL 1.1) — the actual `.ttf` font files
     themselves, originally designed by Christian Robertson and maintained
     by the Google Fonts team as the Roboto Project.

The CodeBrix-authored parts of this package — the `.csproj`, the
buildTransitive `.targets` file, the `.ttf.manifest` JSON, the `.uprimarker`
marker, the documentation, and the packaging metadata — are original to the
CodeBrix family and contain no third-party source code. They are released,
together with the bundled font files, under the SIL Open Font License,
Version 1.1, the same license as the fonts. The full license text is bundled
with this repository as `OFL.txt` at the repository root and is also packaged
inside the produced NuGet under the same name.

   Modifications made during redistribution:
     - Only the six weights Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (500),
       SemiBold (600), Bold (700), and ExtraBold (800) are shipped as static
       instances; the upstream Thin (100), ExtraLight (200), and Black (900)
       static instances are not included. (Those weights remain reachable
       through the bundled variable font.)
     - The upstream upright variable font `Roboto-VariableFont_wdth,wght.ttf`
       was renamed to `Roboto.ttf`. No `.ttf` binary content was altered.
     - The separate upstream italic variable font
       `Roboto-Italic-VariableFont_wdth,wght.ttf` is not included; italic
       styles are provided through the static instances.
